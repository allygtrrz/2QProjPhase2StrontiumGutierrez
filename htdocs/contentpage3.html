japanese imperial demon army

The Japanese Imperial Demon Army is a military group under the Order of the Imperial Demons, a powerful magical syndicate led by the Hiragi Clan. Likewise, the Japanese Imperial Demon Army has been passed on from one Hiragi leader to another. After the apocalypse wiped out most of humanity, the Japanese Imperial Demon Army rose to power as one of the few human organizations left to retaliate against the oppressive vampire race.

moon demon company

The Moon Demon Company is an elite unit of the Japanese Imperial Demon Army's Vampire Extermination Unit. It is led by Lieutenant Colonel Ichinose Guren, who encourages teamwork and familial bonds among the members of the company. Therefore, all members of the company work in squads of 5, all of which act as families of 5 led by their respective sergeants.

Within the Moon Demon Company, there are 3 squads that play a substantial role in the story of Owari no Seraph. These are:

shinoa squad

Sergeant Hiragi Shinoa leads the Shinoa Squad, whose members comprise 5 of the 6 main characters of Owari no Seraph. Albeit young, the members of the Shinoa Squad are all capable of standing their ground in battle. As they all grow individually and as a team, they learn to treat and love one another like family, especially with the help of Shinoa's leadership capabilities.
