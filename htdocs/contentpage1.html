Released Volumes of Owari no Seraph

Owari no Seraph currently has 26 released volumes, with the oldest dating back to January 2013 and the newest being released just this February 2022.

Considering the release dates of these 26 volumes, Owari no Seraph releases a new volume every 4 or 5 months. This suggests a pattern of 1 chapter being released at the beginning of every month, and with each volume consisting of 4 or 5 chapters, this results in the aforementioned 4 or 5 month interval between the 26 volumes, totalling to a time span of 8 years and counting, given that Owari no Seraph is still an ongoing manga.
